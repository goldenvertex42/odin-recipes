Belly Button Eggs

Description

Egg in the hole makes a delicious breakfast! A runny yolk egg cooked in the center of a piece of toast and eaten with a fork is a great way to enjoy an egg in a slightly different way. My kids feel fancy when eating these.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 teaspoons bacon grease
  • 1 slice bread
  • 1 egg
  • salt and ground pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Melt bacon grease in a nonstick pan over low heat.
  2. Cut a 2-inch hole in the center of bread slice; place in the hot skillet.
  3. Cook until lightly toasted - about 2 minutes. Flip bread and crack egg into the hole; season with salt and pepper. Continue to cook until egg is cooked and mostly firm; flip again and cook 1 minute more.
  4. Serve immediately.
